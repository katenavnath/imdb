STARmeter Award

About the Awards

IMDb and IMDbPro present STARmeter "Fan Favorite" and "Breakout Star" awards throughout the year at major entertainment events such as the Sundance Film Festival, Toronto International Film Festival, Cannes Film Festival and Comic-Con International: San Diego.

The awards are based on the IMDbPro exclusive STARmeter rankings chart, which highlight the actors most searched by hundreds of millions of IMDb customers all over the world. IMDb STARmeter Awards have proven to be a keenly accurate predictor of stars who are about to have a breakthrough career moment.
